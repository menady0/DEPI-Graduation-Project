About Us:

Welcome to Coffee Fly, where the rich aromas of freshly brewed coffee and the cool Cairo breeze meet. Nestled in the heart of Zamalek, Coffee Fly is more than just a coffee shop—it’s an experience. Inspired by Egypt’s deep history and vibrant culture, we offer a unique twist on traditional coffeehouse flavors, blending them with global influences. Whether you’re looking for the perfect cup to start your day or a cozy corner to relax in, Coffee Fly takes you on a sensory journey, one cup at a time.

Our Misson:

At Coffee Fly, our mission is simple: to create a space where great coffee, creativity, and community come together. We aim to elevate the coffee experience by offering exceptional, ethically-sourced brews that celebrate the rich flavors of Egypt and the world. With every cup we serve, we strive to inspire connections, spark conversations, and encourage moments of calm in the hustle and bustle of Cairo.

We are committed to crafting an atmosphere that fosters warmth, inclusivity, and creativity—whether you’re here to unwind, work, or simply enjoy the perfect sip. We believe that coffee is more than just a drink; it’s a journey, and at Coffee Fly, we’re here to take you higher.
